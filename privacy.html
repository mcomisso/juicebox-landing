Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 10, 2025

Overview

At JuiceBox, your privacy is our top priority. We are committed to protecting your personal information and being transparent about our data practices. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use the JuiceBox application.

Data Collection

We do not collect any personal data. JuiceBox is designed with privacy at its core:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No advertising or third-party trackers
  • No account creation or login required
  • No server communication or cloud storage

Local Data Storage

All data you create within JuiceBox is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's secure local storage technologies (SwiftData). This includes:

  • Battery device information you manually enter
  • Charge history and tracking data
  • Custom categories and preferences
  • Widget configurations

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

HomeKit Integration

JuiceBox can optionally access your HomeKit accessories to display battery levels. This integration:

  • Requires your explicit permission via iOS system prompts
  • Only accesses battery level information from HomeKit devices
  • Processes all HomeKit data locally on your device
  • Does not transmit any HomeKit data to external servers

You can revoke HomeKit access at any time through your iOS Settings app.

Third-Party Services

JuiceBox does not use any third-party services, analytics tools, or advertising networks. The app operates completely independently and offline.

Data Backup and iCloud

If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device, your JuiceBox data may be included in your encrypted iCloud backup. This backup is:

  • Controlled entirely by your iOS device settings
  • Encrypted by Apple
  • Accessible only by you through your Apple ID
  • Not accessible to JuiceBox developers or MC Software Ltd

Children's Privacy

JuiceBox does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no information for us to access, modify, or delete. You maintain complete control over your data through your device:

  • All data can be deleted by uninstalling the app
  • Individual items can be deleted within the app interface
  • No request to us is necessary to remove your data

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the app and on this website with an updated "Last Updated" date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact Us

  • Website: getjuicebox.app
